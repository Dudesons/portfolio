Continuous Delivery


Continuous Deployment means that every change goes through the pipeline and automatically gets put into production, resulting in many production deployments every day.
Continuous Delivery just means that you are able to do frequent deployments but may choose not to do it, usually due to businesses preferring a slower rate of deployment.
In order to do Continuous Deployment you must be doing Continuous Delivery.
Le Continuous Déployment est une extension du Continuous Delivery où l'ont automatise le déploiement en production d'une release des qu'elle est disponible en production.
Pour faire du Continuous Deployment efficace vous devez avoir mis en place un processus de Continuous Delivery solide.
